Welcome to **Campus Talks**. This platform is a safe space for students to share, connect, and express themselves anonymously. To keep this community healthy and helpful, all members must strictly follow these rules:

## 1. Respect Anonymity
- Do not attempt to "dox" or reveal the identity of any user.
- Respect the pseudo-names assigned. Every "Hidden Owl" and "Quiet Ninja" has a right to their privacy.

## 2. Zero Tolerance for Bullying
- Targeted harassment, hate speech, or bullying of specific individuals (students/staff) is strictly prohibited.
- Content reported for bullying 5 times will be automatically hidden and reviewed.

## 3. Privacy & Safety
- **No PII**: Never post Personal Identifiable Information (phone numbers, private addresses, specific dorm room numbers).
- **Safety**: If you or someone you know is in distress, please use university counseling services. This platform is NOT a replacement for emergency mental health support.

## 4. Academic Integrity
- While discussing courses is encouraged, do not use the platform for cheating or sharing exam questions.

## 5. Content Standards
- No explicit adult content (NSFW).
- No promotion of illegal activities.
- Moderate your language. Our AI filter will automatically clean common profanity.

## 6. User Rights (Edit/Delete)
- You own your voice. You can **Edit** or **Delete** your posts at any time while you are logged in.
- Deleted content is permanently removed from the public feed.
